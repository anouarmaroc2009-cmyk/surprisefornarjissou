HOW TO ADD YOUR PHOTOS
======================

1. Put your pictures in this folder (pictures/)
2. Name them photo1.jpg, photo2.jpg, ... photo8.jpg
   (or edit the filenames in index.html)
3. Supported formats: .jpg, .jpeg, .png, .webp

For the background music:
- Add an MP3 file named "our-song.mp3" to this folder
- Or change the src in index.html to point to your song file

TIPS:
- Use landscape-oriented photos for best results
- Crop to square (1:1) for consistent gallery look
- The first photo (photo1.jpg) will be the most visible one

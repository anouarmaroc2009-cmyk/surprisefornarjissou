HOW TO CUSTOMIZE THIS WEBSITE
=============================

1. ADD YOUR PHOTOS
   - Drop your pictures into the "pictures" folder
   - Name them photo1.jpg through photo8.jpg
   - You can add more by editing the gallery section in index.html

2. ADD YOUR NAMES
   - Open index.html in any text editor
   - Search for "Anouar" and replace with your name
   - Search for "Narjiss" and replace with your girlfriend's name
   - (around line 115 and 225)

3. SET YOUR ANNIVERSARY DATE
   - Open index.html
   - Search for "2026-04-04" (line ~284)
   - Change it to the date you two became official
   - Format: YYYY-MM-DD

4. EDIT THE LOVE STORY
   - In the timeline section (around line 160),
     update the events, dates, and descriptions to match your story

5. ADD A SONG (optional)
   - Drop an MP3 file named "our-song.mp3" into the pictures folder
   - The music button in the bottom-right will play it

6. CUSTOMIZE THE REASONS
   - Around line 190, you'll find the "Why I Love You" cards
   - Change the text to your own personal reasons

7. PUBLISH ONLINE (free)
   - Upload the entire "for-you" folder to:
     * GitHub Pages (free) — instructions: https://pages.github.com
     * Netlify (free) — drag & drop the folder
     * Or just open index.html directly in a browser!

Enjoy making her smile!
